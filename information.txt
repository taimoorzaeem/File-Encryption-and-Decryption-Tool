Unlike the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), which is a domestic security service, the CIA has no law enforcement function and is officially mainly focused on overseas intelligence gathering, with only limited domestic intelligence collection.[8] The CIA serves as the national manager for the coordination of HUMINT activities across the U.S. intelligence community.
